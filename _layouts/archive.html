This is my portfolio from June 2019 to January 2021. It is available on the public web, but intended specifically for the DMA committee at the Yale School of Music. Members of the committee can navigate this site in two ways:

  • Click any item, then use the "previous" and "next" links at the bottom of the page.
  • Return to this page by clicking "Nate May, composer and educator" at the top.

Some items have "private links" to scores, programs, etc. When following these links, you may be prompted to log in to Google. All links will be viewable if you are logged into a Yale account.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

-Nate

The Midnight Oil Collective is a creator-led venture studio that is creating a new model for arts funding in the United States. I am one of nine co-founders and currently serve on the board. In addition to helping steer the group, I have contributed in the areas of graphic design, marketing, video editing, cybersecurity, social media, web, management, and other areas.

Midnight Oil Collective has been supported by the Tsai Center for Innovative Thinking at Yale, COLLAB New Haven, and the Innovation and Innovation Clinic at Yale Law.

We run an incubator and accelerator, and some of the projects we have helped include TV shows, operas, theatrical projects, a record label, a film, and a publishing group.

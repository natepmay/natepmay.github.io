--- title: "\\xfb$" tags: artistic duration: 9 min. premieredate: December 5, 2019 premiereensemble: The Montclair State Electronic Music Ensemble premierevenue: The George Segal Gallery, Montclair, NJ instrumentation: 7 laptops ---

Taking its name from a set of characters that appeared when I accidentally "printed" the contents of a digital recording of Beethoven's Fifth Symphony to my computer screen, this piece examines the machine understanding of human understand of machines. I created the piece using a tool I created called the Soundspitter, which chops up sound samples into tiny parts and arranges them according to user-defined parameters that are "taught" to the program via machine learning. Among the parameters used were "tonelessness" and "anthropomorphism."

Synthase is an online school that I founded for open-minded music creators.

Here are the major projects that Synthase has undertaken:

  • Isn't Music Theory (YouTube Series)
  • The Synthase Guide to Chords
  • Chord Candy (online course)
  • The Keyring (web app)
  • Chordle (learning game)
  • Individual composition lessons

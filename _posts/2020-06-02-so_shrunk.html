--- title: So Shrunk My Sinews tags: artistic duration: 6 min. instrumentation: bitKlavier commissioner: Dan Trueman ---

So Shrunk My Sinews was one of two pieces I wrote on commission from Princeton professor Dan Trueman for his software instrument, the bitKlavier. The compositional process involved both composing notes for the performer to play and designing and programming the patch, which evolves as the pieces develops.
